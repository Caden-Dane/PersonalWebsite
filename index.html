A masterpiece that transformed how I think about writing maintainable, readable code. Essential reading for any serious developer.

Revolutionary insights on building good habits and breaking bad ones. This book changed how I approach personal and professional growth.

A fascinating exploration of human history that provides valuable context for understanding technology's role in society.

Mindset
